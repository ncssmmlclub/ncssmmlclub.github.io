We are the machine learning club at the North Carolina School of Science and Mathematics. Through our organization, we give engaging lecures, host school-wide hackathons, organize speaker series, and beyond! We have two primary goals: to make artificial intelligence more accessible and to teach others how to use it to solve complex problems in society.
